Support article: Login and account access issues

Customers may report login and account access problems such as:
- cannot log in
- login not working
- log in problem
- sign in failing
- unable to access account
- forgot password
- forgot login password
- password reset not working
- password reset email not received
- browser cache problems
- clear browser cache
- login page keeps refreshing
- stuck after sign in
- expired session issues
- account access issue

Recommended troubleshooting steps:
1. Ask the customer to try a password reset.
2. Ask the customer to check spam or junk folders for the password reset email.
3. Ask the customer to clear browser cache and cookies.
4. Ask the customer to close the browser and try again.
5. Ask the customer to try signing in from a fresh session or another browser.
6. If the issue started after an update, collect the browser, device, and exact login behavior before escalation.

If access issues continue, collect the account email, workspace if relevant, browser details, and exact error behavior before escalation.

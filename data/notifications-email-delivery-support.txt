Support article: Notifications and email delivery issues

Customers may report notification and email delivery problems such as:
- not receiving email
- notification not received
- verification email missing
- reset email missing
- invite email not arriving
- email delayed
- email went to spam
- notification settings not working
- alerts not sending
- reminder email not received
- digest email missing
- email delivery issue

Recommended troubleshooting steps:
1. Ask the customer to check spam, junk, and promotions folders.
2. Confirm the email address on the account is correct.
3. Ask whether email notifications are enabled in notification settings.
4. Confirm whether the message was delayed or never arrived.
5. If only one type of email is missing, identify whether it is a verification, reset, reminder, invite, or digest email.
6. If needed, collect the approximate send time and recipient address before escalation.

If the issue continues, collect the account email, notification type, workspace if relevant, and expected send time before escalation.

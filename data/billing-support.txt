Support article: Billing and subscription issues

Customers may report billing and subscription problems such as:
- charged twice
- duplicate charge
- invoice not available
- payment failed
- credit card declined
- subscription did not renew
- plan downgrade not reflected
- plan upgrade not reflected
- billing portal not loading
- trial expired unexpectedly
- auto-renew confusion
- refund request
- cancellation question

Recommended troubleshooting steps:
1. Confirm which workspace or account the billing issue is related to.
2. Ask the customer to review the latest invoice and billing history.
3. Confirm whether the payment method is valid and not expired.
4. Ask whether the customer recently upgraded, downgraded, or canceled the plan.
5. If there is a duplicate charge, collect invoice IDs and payment timestamps before escalating.
6. If the billing portal is not loading, ask the customer to refresh, try another browser, or clear cache.

If billing behavior looks incorrect, collect invoice details, subscription plan, workspace ID, and payment timing before escalation.
